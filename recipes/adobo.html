ADOBO

Description

A staple savory dish of every filipino family.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs Meat of your choice
    • Chicken
    • Pork
  • 1 Whole Garlic (crushed)
  • Dried Basil Leaf
  • Whole Peppercorns
  • 3 Tablespoon Sugar
  • 1/2 cup Soy sauce
  • 4 Tablespoon Vinegar

Steps

  1. Get a large enough pot for the amount of meat you have.
  2. Take your garlic and break them into seperate cloves. With the use of a knife, flat against the garlic, pound each garlic and peel their skin. Set aside all the peeled garlic.
  3. Get a large enough pot for the amount of meat you have
  4. Put all the following ingredients in the pot.
    • The meat of your choice
    • Garlic
    • Dried bay leaves
    • Sugar
    • Soy sauce
    • Vinegar
  5. Bring it to a boil on high heat.
  6. Once it boils, bring to low heat and let simmer for 20 to 30 minutes or until meat is tender.
  7. Make sure to taste after!
  8. Add sugar or salt to taste
  9. Done! You just made Adobo. Pair it with white rice. Enjoy!
